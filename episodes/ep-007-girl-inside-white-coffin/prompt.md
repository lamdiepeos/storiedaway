Ahead, Daniel faces his daughter's closed white coffin covered in lilies; Vanessa waits beneath a black umbrella beside him, while two security guards watch the grave. Static wide shot from coffin level, wet earth, gray sky, raw film grain, sharp contrast between formal grief and the desperate outsider, highly detailed.
