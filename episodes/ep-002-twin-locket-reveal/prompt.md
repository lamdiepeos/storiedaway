# Production Prompt Hub (ep-002)

## 1. Keyframe Image Prompt (Streamlined)
Cinematic realism, 9:16 vertical frame, cold late-afternoon daylight. Elegant 30s mother Victoria in a camel wool coat walks briskly hand-in-hand with her 11-year-old son Daniel in a crisp navy school uniform on a crowded upscale Manhattan sidewalk. Polished storefronts, luxury cars, yellow taxis, affluent pedestrians. Wide lateral tracking shot, movie lighting, raw film grain, highly detailed.

## 2. 10-Second Video Prompt (Streamlined)
Wide lateral tracking shot, camera moves with Victoria and Daniel, 9:16 vertical, no cuts.
- **0s-3s (Setting & Setup):** Victoria in a camel coat walks briskly holding hands with Daniel in a navy school uniform on a busy upscale NYC sidewalk. The homeless boy remains off-screen. *(Matches the Keyframe Image perfectly).*
- **3s-6s (Action & Dialogue):** At 3s, Daniel jerks his hand free, stops, and points off-screen. At 4s, Daniel whispers audio dialogue: "Mom... that boy has my face." Victoria turns sharply, her face freezing in shock. Camera stops with them.
- **6s-10s (The Twist & Locked Peak):** At 6s, camera pans toward a weathered lamppost and slowly pushes in. Reveal a dirty barefoot boy looking up with Daniel's exact face, eyes, and dimple, but hollow cheeks and torn clothes. At 7s, a tarnished bronze half-locket glints against his chest. Video ends on the twins' locked, stunned stare.
- **Sound:** Loud NYC street ambience from 0s-3s, abruptly fading to total dead silence at 4s exactly on Daniel's whisper. No music.
