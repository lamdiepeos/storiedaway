# Production Prompt Hub (ep-003)

## 1. Keyframe Image Prompt (Streamlined)
Cinematic realism, 9:16 vertical frame, cold gray daylight. Seven-year-old Emily in a white coat and blue hair bow stands inside a rain-dark brick alley, holding a wrapped sandwich toward a thin homeless boy seated against the wall in loose gray clothes. The boy watches the sandwich with dirty hands held close to his body. Claire remains off-screen. Static wide shot, wet pavement, muted city background, movie lighting, raw film grain, sharp contrast between childhood comfort and street hunger, highly detailed.

## 2. 10-Second Video Prompt (Cinematic Multi-Scene Drama)
**Global Style:** Cinematic live-action realism, 9:16 vertical frame, raw film grain, high-tension psychological drama, intense color grading, shallow depth of field.

**Timeline & Scene Breakdown:**
- **0s-3s (Scene 1 - The Confrontation/Opening Beat):** Static wide shot, eye level. Emily in a white coat and blue bow faces thin homeless boy Liam seated against a wet brick wall in a rain-dark alley, holding a wrapped sandwich toward him. At 1.5s, Emily delivers Audio Dialogue: "You can have it." Sound: urgent rain, distant traffic, quick city footsteps.
- **3s-6s (Scene 2 - The Escalation/Hard Cut):** Hard cut to extreme close-up on Claire's tense hands pulling Emily backward by the shoulders. Claire's fearful face fills the frame as Liam freezes with the sandwich clutched to his chest. At 4.5s, Claire delivers Audio Dialogue: "Emily, step back!" Sound: sharp fabric pull, handbag buckle strike, breathless gasp.
- **6s-10s (Scene 3 - The Climax/The Twist Reveal):** Hard cut to tight alternating close-ups of Claire and Liam. Claire sees Liam's face, turns pale, and drops her handbag onto the wet pavement. Liam raises his frightened eyes and recognizes her. At 8s, Liam delivers Audio Dialogue: "Mom?" Video ends on their sharp, dramatic locked stare.
- **Sound Specification:** Multi-layered rain, traffic, footsteps, fabric movement, and handbag impact. Sudden dramatic stings on both hard cuts, breathing gasps, alley ambience fading into dead silence on Liam's final whisper. No music.
