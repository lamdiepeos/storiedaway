# Production Prompt Hub (ep-004)

## Keyframe Image Prompt
Cinematic realism, 9:16 vertical frame, cold blue evening beneath the golden hotel canopy. At the exact 0-second frame, six-year-old Ethan in a navy blazer kneels one arm's length from a thin homeless boy seated against the outer marble wall in a torn gray hooded jacket. Ethan holds a closed paper lunch bag on his knee; the boy looks down with empty hands tucked beneath his arms, unaware of the food. Caroline, wealthy guests, and photographers remain several meters behind near velvet ropes. Static wide shot, crystal light through glass doors, raw film grain, sharp contrast between luxury and hunger, highly detailed.

## 15-Second Video Prompt
Cinematic trailer, 9:16, five distinct scenes, dramatic hard cuts, cold luxury-thriller realism. 0-3s: starting exactly from the keyframe, Ethan opens the paper bag, extends the sandwich to the seated boy, and says, "Here. You can have my sandwich." 3-6s: hard cut to Caroline forcing through wealthy guests as she shouts, "Ethan, wait!" and sees the boy's face. 6-9s: hard cut between the boys' identical eyes and matching dimples as phones rise and a guest gasps, "They look like twins!" 9-12s: hard cut to a faded hospital bracelet marked WARD, INFANT B; Caroline drops her handbag and says, "They told me only one baby survived." 12-15s: hard cut to the boy's wet eyes as he asks, "Then why did you leave me?" Caroline collapses as the screen cuts to black. Sound: camera shutters, crowd laughter breaking into gasps, handbag impact, rising heartbeat, and sudden silence after the final question. Music: dark strings and low pulses building into epic percussion, ducking beneath dialogue, then stopping for one deep final impact.
